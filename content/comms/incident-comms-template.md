# Incident Communication Templates

Three templates for different stages of an incident.
Replace all [BRACKETED] fields before sending.

---

## Template 1: Initial Notification (First 30 minutes)

**To**: [Leadership / Stakeholders]
**Subject**: [SECURITY INCIDENT] [Brief description] -- [Date]
**Classification**: [SENSITIVE / INTERNAL]

---

We are investigating a potential security incident. Here is what we know at this time.

**What happened**: [Brief, plain-language description of the event]

**When detected**: [Date/Time]

**Systems affected**: [System name(s) or "Under investigation"]

**Current status**: Active investigation in progress.

**Immediate actions taken**:
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]

**What we don't know yet**: [Be honest -- unknown scope, unknown cause, etc.]

**Next update**: [Specific time -- e.g., "By 3:00 PM today"]

**Point of contact**: Roze San Nicolas | [contact info]

---

## Template 2: Situation Update (Ongoing)

**To**: [Leadership / Stakeholders]
**Subject**: [UPDATE #X] [SECURITY INCIDENT] [Brief description] -- [Date]

---

**Update as of**: [Date/Time]

**Current status**: [ INVESTIGATING | CONTAINED | REMEDIATING | RESOLVED ]

**What we've confirmed since last update**:
- [Finding 1]
- [Finding 2]

**Scope**:
- Systems affected: [List]
- Data involved: [Yes/No -- describe if yes]
- Users affected: [Number / None confirmed]

**Actions in progress**:
- [Action 1 -- Owner -- ETA]
- [Action 2 -- Owner -- ETA]

**Open questions**:
- [What we're still investigating]

**Next update**: [Specific time]
