## PROJECT OVERVIEW

### What is HITRUST r2?
- **Gold standard** for security certification in federal and healthcare sectors
- **2-year validated assessment** conducted by external auditor (A-LIGN)
- **Prescriptive framework** covering 19 control categories with hundreds of specific requirements
- **Risk-based approach** that aligns with NIST, ISO, and federal security requirements

### Strategic Value
- **Competitive advantage** in federal contracting and partnerships
- **Single framework** that satisfies multiple compliance obligations (FedRAMP, FISMA, NIST)
- **Reduced audit burden** through consolidated evidence collection
- **Market differentiator** demonstrating security maturity to stakeholders

## CRITICAL GAPS & RISKS

### Phase 1 Priorities (Must Complete by April 30)

1. **AI Governance Policy** ⚠️ CRITICAL
   - **Gap:** No formal AI governance framework exists
   - **Impact:** Blocks 40+ control requirements
   - **Action Required:** Policy development and board approval
   - **Resource Need:** Legal review, stakeholder alignment

2. **AI Risk Assessment Process** ⚠️ CRITICAL
   - **Gap:** No structured process for AI system risk evaluation
   - **Impact:** Cannot demonstrate AI lifecycle management
   - **Action Required:** Implement Vanta AI Risk Assessment module
   - **Resource Need:** 40 hours implementation effort

3. **AI Asset Inventory** 🔶 HIGH
   - **Gap:** Incomplete catalog of AI systems (Vertex AI/Gemini in scope)
   - **Impact:** Cannot define assessment boundary
   - **Action Required:** Complete AI system discovery and documentation
   - **Resource Need:** Cross-team collaboration

4. **AI Model Change Management** 🔶 HIGH
   - **Gap:** No formal change control for AI model updates
   - **Impact:** Cannot demonstrate configuration management
   - **Action Required:** Extend Jira workflows for AI changes
   - **Resource Need:** Process design and training
