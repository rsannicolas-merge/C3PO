## LinkedIn Post Formula

**Hook** (1-2 lines -- stop the scroll)
> Bold claim, provocative question, or surprising stat. No fluff.

**The Real Talk** (3-5 lines)
> What actually happened / what you actually learned. Be specific. Name the problem.

**The Insight** (2-3 lines)
> What it means for federal security teams. What most people get wrong.

**The Takeaway** (1-2 lines)
> One concrete thing the reader can do or think differently about.

**CTA** (optional)
> "Drop your take below." / "What's your approach?"
