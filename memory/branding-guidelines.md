## Typography

### Primary Typeface - Fraunces
**Fraunces** is a display, soft-serif typeface inspired by early 20th century typefaces with distinct curves evoking friendly sophistication.

#### Weights Available
- Fraunces Light (for extended multi-line headlines)
- Fraunces Regular
- Fraunces Medium (for short, impactful headlines - 2 words or less)
- Fraunces SemiBold
- ❌ Never use Fraunces Black (except for names in bios at small sizes)

#### Usage Guidelines
- **Headlines & Impact Copy**: Primary use case
- **Tracking**: -10 to -20 in design software, -0.02em for web
- **Kerning**: Use "Optical" instead of "Auto" or "Metrics"
- **Size/Weight Ratio**: Larger type = thinner weight, smaller type = heavier weight
- **Leading**: Keep tight without overlapping ascenders/descenders
- **Color Mix**: Can combine Viridian Green and Dark Green for emphasis
- ❌ **Never use in uppercase**
- ❌ **Never use Black weight** (except specific bio cases)

### Secondary Typeface - Epilogue
**Epilogue** is a versatile modern sans-serif with crisp geometric features and humanist warmth. Open, rounded shapes with low contrast provide excellent readability.

#### Weights Available (All include italic variants)
- Epilogue Regular (body copy)
- Epilogue Medium
- Epilogue SemiBold
- Epilogue Bold
- Epilogue ExtraBold
- Epilogue Small Caps (available in all weights)

#### Usage Guidelines
- **Body Copy**: Epilogue Regular - no additional leading/kerning needed
- **Subheads**: Epilogue Medium Small Caps
- **Capitalization**: Use Small Caps, never ALL CAPS/uppercase
- **Letter Spacing**: Can increase for capitalized subheads/short copy

#### Accessing Small Caps
- **Adobe CC**: Character Palette › Small Caps
- **Figma**: Type Settings › "Forced small caps"
- **Google Docs/Slides**: Not supported - avoid capitalization
- **CSS/HTML**: `font-family: Epilogue; font-variant: small-caps;`

#### Color Distribution

#### Gradients
Used subtly as vignettes only. Apply Dark Green over Warm Gray background:
- Upper-left: 15% opacity
- Lower-right: 25% opacity
- Apply with soft brush in Photoshop

Approved gradient combinations:
- Dark Green → Forest Green
- Dark Green → Forest Green → Viridian Green

#### WCAG Accessibility Compliance
Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 (normal text), 3:1 (large text 18px+)

**Passing Combinations** (✓):
- White over Viridian Green: 4.59
- Electrolight Green over Forest Green: 4.71
- Electrolight Green over Dark Green: 6.42
- Viridian Green over Warm Gray: 3.75
- Medium Gray over Warm Gray: 4.83
- Dark Green over Warm Gray: 10.07
- White over Teal: 3.00 (large text only)
- White over Deep Sea Blue: 4.68
- Warm Gray over Deep Sea Blue: 3.82
- Warm Gray over Navy Blue: 13.2
- Electrolight Green over Navy Blue: 8.42
- Teal over Navy Blue: 5.38
- Deep Sea Blue over Navy Blue: 3.45

**Failing Combinations** (✗):
- White over Electrolight Green: 1.91
- Warm Gray over Teal: 2.29

## Documentation Standards

### File Headers
All primary documentation should include:
```markdown
# [Title] - C3PO Security Operations Command Center

**[Descriptive subtitle explaining purpose]**

---
```

### Section Formatting
- Use emoji consistently per the system above
- Bold key terms on first use
- Include confidence ratings for assessments
- Add evidence quality levels for claims

### Compliance References
When referencing frameworks:
- **HITRUST r2 v11.5**: Always include version
- **HITRUST AI2**: Specify as AI-specific assessment
- **NIST 800-53**: Include revision number
- **FedRAMP**: Specify level (Low/Moderate/High)
